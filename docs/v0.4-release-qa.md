## Residual Risks

- The dogfood used deterministic bash-backed `millrace_agent` sessions rather
  than live LLM operator sessions.
- Interactive scroll/search/copy is still primarily proven by automated cockpit
  fixtures and prior evidence, not by a newly recorded human terminal session.
- The context export is intentionally bounded. It references raw artifacts by
  path/checksum rather than embedding full PTY logs, raw replay rings, or full
  screen snapshots.
- macOS and SSH evidence must be refreshed before making broad terminal-matrix
  claims for v0.4.
